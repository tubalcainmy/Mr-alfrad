Why Property Owners Choose CEA

Built for trust, not just listings

Every safeguard below exists because fraud is the single biggest barrier to a healthy property market — we designed it out.

ID

Verified Identity, Every Time

NIN and BVN checks plus a liveness selfie confirm the person listing is who they say they are — before a listing ever goes live.

📄

Title Documents Checked by Humans

Our verification team manually reviews title documents and cross-checks coordinates against AGIS and state GIS records — not just an algorithm.

🔒

Escrow-Protected Payments

Buyer funds sit in a CEA escrow account and are only released to the seller once document transfer is confirmed.

⚠

Active Fraud Detection

Duplicate NIN/BVN checks and coordinate-proximity alerts automatically flag suspicious listings for manual review before they publish.

✅

The CEA Verified Badge

Only listings that pass full verification carry the badge — giving genuine buyers a clear, trustworthy signal at a glance.

☎

A Real Team Behind Every Listing

Disputes are escalated to CEA's Chief Legal Officer within 48 hours, with a formal mediation process — you're never on your own.
